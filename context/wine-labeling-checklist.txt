CHECKLIST OF MANDATORY LABEL INFORMATION | WINE
This guidance lists the mandatory information that TTB reviews on every wine label and certificate of label
approval application and includes a checklist to help prevent common errors. This document does not
provide comprehensive labeling guidance. Wine for which a certificate of label approval is required must
be labeled in compliance with all applicable requirements in 27 CFR part 4 and 27 CFR part 16.
